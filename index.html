My Experience As A Pianist

I began playing the piano in grade school and took lessons for a few years. I took a break from the piano to transition my time and focus into participating in the extra curricular activity of playing baseball. During the lockdown in 2020, I found myself looking for a hobby that would keep me occupied in the state of being at home.

I chose to start playing piano again. After learning each piece I wanted to play, I determined it would be fun to document each score's performance on video. These videos started out as more of a personal trophy collection, but at some point I figured that there may be others that would enjoy these videos as well. If you do decide to view this content, I hope you enjoy, and thanks for watching!
